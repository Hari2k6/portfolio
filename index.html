AI-Based Blend Optimization Tool

Built an innovative prototype for optimizing chemical blends, leveraging real-world experimental data to meet specific target properties. This AI-driven tool applies advanced optimization algorithms to intelligently balance complex cost and property constraints, such as viscosity, density, and purity. Key features include lot-level refinement, allowing for precise adjustments based on batch variations, and real-time visualization of optimization results, providing immediate feedback and enabling informed decision-making for chemical engineers. This project highlights my ability to apply AI to solve complex industrial problems.
